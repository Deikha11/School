About Us

Vision

Darunasaih School aspires to be a leader in education, fostering generations who become impactful contributors to their society and the world, equipped with modern knowledge and sound Islamic character.

Mission

Our mission is to provide students with high-quality integrated education, utilizing a comprehensive curriculum that develops knowledge, skills, personality, and Islamic values. We create an inspiring, safe learning environment that allows every student to reach their full potential.

History & Values

Darunasaih School was founded in [Year of Establishment, e.g., 2005] in response to the need for a school offering high-quality education rooted in Somali and Islamic culture and values. Our core values include:

  • **Ethics:** Promoting good manners and respect.
  • **Knowledge:** The continuous pursuit of knowledge and understanding.
  • **Innovation:** Fostering creative thinking and problem-solving.
  • **Collaboration:** Working together to achieve common goals.
  • **Responsibility:** Instilling personal and social responsibility.

Our Academic Programs

We offer a comprehensive and integrated curriculum designed to equip students with knowledge and skills for a bright future.

Primary School

Grades 1 to 8. Focus on foundational knowledge, writing, reading, mathematics, and religious studies.

Secondary School

Grades 9 to 12. A comprehensive curriculum preparing students for university and life.

Integrated Curriculum

Combining Islamic studies with modern subjects like Somali, Arabic, English, Sciences, Mathematics, and Technology.

Our Curriculum

Our curriculum is integrated, including both Islamic and modern subjects. We ensure that students receive comprehensive knowledge that prepares them for the future.

  • Islamic Studies
  • Languages (Somali, Arabic, English)
  • Sciences (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
  • Mathematics
  • Social Studies
  • Technology

Enrollment Requirements

  • Two passport-sized photographs.
  • Copy of birth certificate.
  • Copy of previous school report card (if applicable).
  • Certificate of good conduct (from previous school, if applicable).
